About From Paws to Ears

This site shares what we've learned raising Zeus (Doberman), Simba (Pomeranian), and Gandalf (Lionhead Dwarf Rabbit). Our goal is to help families understand health, temperament, diet, and hygiene for each pet—without the fluff (okay, except Simba's).

Explore each guide for practical tips, vet-informed checklists, and everyday routines that keep our trio happy and healthy.
